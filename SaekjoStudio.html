A set of four characters and a sidekick mascot, each designed to represent a Korean personal color season (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter), giving Saekjo Studio — the first Korean Personal Color Studio in Surabaya — a distinct face for every palette.

  • Role Character Design
    Mascot Design
    Character Animation
  • Project Span 5 Months
  • Softwares
  • Target Medium Brand Mascots

Background

Korean personal color analysis is a growing beauty and fashion trend, but it can be hard for people to visualize and relate to each seasonal type. Saekjo Studio, Surabaya's first Korean personal color studio, needed characters that could bring these four palettes to life in a way that feels personal and memorable.

Design Objective

To design representative mascots and characters that communicate each Korean personal color type clearly, helping Saekjo Studio build a stronger, more distinctive brand identity that their audience can connect with.

Main Concept

Gems of Korea

한국의 보석

The main concept behind creating Saekjo Studio's characters and mascots is the idea of standing out in a beautiful and authentic way. Just like a gem hidden among plain grey rocks, Saekjo Studio is dedicated to revealing the unique beauty within every person. Everyone deserves to feel special, and being Special is everyone's right.

This idea shapes the character's personality and visual identity, encouraging people to shine by embracing.

Mascot Design

Spring is brought to life through bright, warm colors and an expressive hairstyle that feels youthful without being childish. Her design captures an extroverted warmth — the kind of person who walks into a room and instantly makes it feel lighter, like sunshine finding its way through a field of flowers.

Summer is built around soft, cool tones and a clean, put together look that feels effortlessly elegant without trying too hard. Her design carries a quiet romantic energy, the kind of person who is deeply devoted and a little dramatic about it, someone whose feelings are always just beneath the surface.

Autumn is designed with deep, rich tones and a polished silhouette that radiates luxury and authority without saying a word. Her design carries the energy of someone who has always known her worth, elegant, dominant, and effortlessly commanding, the kind of presence that fills a room before she even walks in.

Winter is built around dark, bold tones and a sharp, unconventional style that sets her apart from everyone else in the room. Her design carries a cold and mysterious energy, the kind of person who does not chase trends but defines them, untouchable, charismatic, and completely in a league of her own.

Starting from nature, animals with unique and extraordinary color identities are explored, eventually leading to the peacock mantis shrimp as the most compelling choice to develop further.

This phase is about finding the right form, exploring and combining shapes while drawing from the iconic traits of the peacock mantis shrimp combined with a Korean touch to build a mascot that is both unique and instantly recognizable.

Saekjuwon comes to life through a range of expressions and behaviors that show exactly who this sidekick is within the Saekjo Studio world, helpful, dedicated to finding the right personal color for everyone, and just a little bit funny when the moment calls for it.

Team

Map

All the Hyang-gi girls originate from different regions across the Republic of Korea, as shown on the map. Each city contributes unique characteristics that shape their lifestyle and the vibe they carry.

Seoul

The capital of South Korea, famous for its nightlife with endless neon lights lining the streets, filled with bars and karaoke spots, is the perfect place for Yejun Floe.

Gangneung

A city in the eastern part of South Korea, is less crowded yet still developed with many universities. Its small village feel, influenced by traditional Korean villages far from the capital, perfectly suits the vibe of Jionha Pearl.

Busan

One of the major cities in the southern part of South Korea, is iconic for its bustling business scene, with skyscrapers, factories, and busy streets filled with people. It perfectly suits Daewona Claire as a hardworking businesswoman and commanding boss.

Jeju-Do

A small island in the southern part of the Korean Peninsula, is full of nature, especially beaches and parks. Its calm and quiet atmosphere, where the sounds of birds fill the air, makes it a perfect place for Soyuna Pippi to take a break from reality.

Saek-juwon originates from the colorful and diverse coral reefs of the Pacific Ocean but accidentally ended up on land in Korea by boat. He now enjoys helping out at Saekjo Studios.
