Background

Indonesia holds some of the world's most extraordinary natural wonders, yet many remain unrecognized and at risk. Nusakala is built around the idea that Indonesian nature is not just beautiful, it is unbeatable, and worth fighting for.

Design Objective

To create an IP for a video game that raises awareness of Indonesia's extraordinary natural wonders from east to west, through five guardians whose powers reflect the raw, unbeatable force of nature itself.

Insight

Designing through the lens of real-world environments proved that observation is as vital as imagination. Grounding characters in specific locations and phenomena ensures a level of authenticity and distinction that abstract design cannot replicate

MAIN CONCEPT

The concept begins with Indonesia itself, studying each natural site for what makes it truly unique, its visual identity, its energy, and its story, before translating those qualities into character design. There are currently 5 sites referred as the project expands to uncover more of the Indonesia's most fascinating nature.

Every extraordinary place needs a guardian, and Indonesia's natural wonders are no exception. Illegal littering, deforestation, and years of irresponsible actions have pushed these places to their limits, and that is where Nusakala (nusa: island, kala: eternity) rises as guardians of the archipelago.

EXPLORATION

The design draws from each environment's unique characteristics, merging nature with fantasy to form a powerful guardian. Every character carries a crown, symbolizing their dominance and their responsibility as the eternal protector of their place.

CHARACTER DESIGN

TEAM

When the five powerful protectors unite, Nusakala becomes a singular unit dedicated to preserving Indonesia's nature. These founding members support one another to protect the environment for the future. This team will continue to expand as more guardians join the cause.

MAP

A visual guide to the origins of the Nusakala Guardians. This map shows the current members and their home regions which can be found across the region along with upcoming members which are currently still lost in signal.
