PORTFOLIO ILLUSTRATION & CONCEPT

The portfolio takes the theme of a game shop, a place where every project acts as a title waiting to be picked up and played. Because a portfolio should feel like somewhere you actually want to spend time and have a great experience. Meanwhile, Laura is always at the counter, happy to show you around and find something worth your while.

Now the game shop is packed. Regulars, newcomers, all drawn in by something they can't quite name, and not one of them is leaving without something they love. And there's still Laura, serving them all with her gorgeous smile. Because a great game always belongs to someone, just like every design project here was made for the right collaborator.

PRINTED BOOK B5

The book is created in B5 size for a comfortable reading experience, packed with a fair amount of pages featuring selected works alongside their process of thinking, designing, and final results. Currently, the book runs at 63 pages, excluding the cover and table of content.

CASE & DVD

This presentation concept reimagines the professional portfolio as a classic physical game release, utilizing the iconic format of a console DVD case. The exterior features a high-impact illustration on the cover and a detailed content preview on the back, while the spine displays the "Portfolio" like those of video game titles.

The interior includes a specialized sleeve for the DVD containing the contact information, alongside a printed B5 portfolio book. This book acts like a premium game manual, housing all works and content in a high-quality physical format that completes the unboxing experience.

The DVD contains hidden features for laptop DVD players that include the digital version of the portfolio book. While some laptops may not work properly to play the disc, it is a tribute to the old style of those good old days of playing video games from a DVD.

WEBSITE

This is the behind the scenes of the website layout, placing every project where it belongs and making the navigation feel easy. The website features idle animations, gif animations, and work timelapses, all things best felt when you're actually there browsing through.

This is the animated homepage, where Laura welcomes you warmly at her game shop with a personality all her own before exploring deeper and discovering the best design projects created by Kevin Macario Gunawan, each with their own unique approach and story to tell.

The website also features a special profile section styled like a character select screen straight out of a video game. Each character of the designer has its own story and personality which most of them are still waiting to be unlocked.
