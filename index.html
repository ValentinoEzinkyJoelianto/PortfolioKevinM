KEVIN MACARIO GUNAWAN

Visual Artist & Concept Designer

Coming from his hometown in Surabaya, Kevin is a Visual Communication Design student at Universitas Ciputra Surabaya with a passion for building worlds, characters, and ideas from scratch. He has worked on several design projects, always making sure his works are not just visually distinct, but also able to present and communicate clearly to the audience. His design works are inspired by the things we encounter in every day life to the rich visual worlds of video games that have shaped his creative perspective.
